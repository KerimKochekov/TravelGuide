Travlmate is a site designed to help eager travellers understand a little bit more about destinations they are going to. Here a traveller can easily access key information about their country of choice including its currency and rates, neighbouring countries and availability of drinking water. Designed as an aid to help with trip planning and organisation, Travlmate is the ideal travel companion. Keep your favourite destinations at reach with our handy bookmark feature!
